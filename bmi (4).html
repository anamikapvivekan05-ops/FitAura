Understanding Your BMI

Underweight
BMI below 18.5
Consider a weight gain plan with calorie-dense, nutritious foods.
Normal Weight ✅
BMI 18.5 – 24.9
Great! Maintain with a balanced diet and regular activity.
Overweight
BMI 25 – 29.9
A calorie-deficit diet with increased activity can help.
Obese
BMI 30 and above
Consult a healthcare professional alongside a weight loss plan.
